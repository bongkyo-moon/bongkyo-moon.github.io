Bongkyo Moon received Ph.D. degree in Telecommunications from KCL (King’s College London), London, UK, in 2004, MSc. degree in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science from GIST, Korea, in 1998, and BSc. degree in Computer Science from Sogang University, Korea, in 1992. He worked as a senior researcher in global standardization research at Telecommunication R&D Centre, Samsung Electronics, Korea, from 2003 to 2005. He also worked as a researcher in system software and enterprise platform both at Inex Technologies, Inc., Santa Clara, CA, USA (including KAIST, Daejeon, Korea) from 1992 to 1996 and at ETRI, Korea, from 1998 to 2000, respectively. Since 2005, he has been working as faculty member in dept. of Computer Science and Engineering, Dongguk University, Seoul, Korea. His research interests are IoT, 5G, Mobile & Cloud Computing, System & Network Security, and Quantum Computing.
