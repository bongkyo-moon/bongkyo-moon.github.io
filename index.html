About the Laboratory

Welcome to the Ubiquitous Computing and Security (UCS) Laboratory of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Dongguk University, Seoul, Korea. The University is located in the centre of Seoul (Chung-Mu-Ro) which is the mecca of Korea film industry. Most of the famous Korean cultures and arts are easily accessible from around the University. A lot of "Korean Wave, Hallyu" movie stars and K-pop stars are attending the Univerisity or gradutes from the University.

For Korean Graduate Students

Supervisor will provide one-to-one tutoring service to all graduate students according to their personal characteristics and academic backgrounds. Cadidate can consult on the course works to be taken in Master Course via department. During writing up his/her MS or Ph.D. thesis, student will overcome the limit of your ability and can achieve great job in his/her research area.

For International Graduate Students

UCS laboratory welcomes inquiries regarding admissions for master or Ph.D course from students of China, Mongolia, Vietnam, Pakistan, Iran, India, Russia, Uzbekistan, Chile, African countries and any other countries. Candidate may contact Dr. Bongkyo Moon with inquiry concerning admission, scholarships, and expenses. You can also find more detail information about scholarship for excellent international students.

As international students are accepted case by case with flexibility of personal backgrounds, it is important that applications are mailed at least a few months in advance. Ordinarily the foreign applicant is not required to take the entrance examination or its equivalent. However, it is essential that all applicants present the evidence of English or Korean language competency and satisfactory or better performance in their previous studies.

UCS Lab - Research Topics

The UCS laboratory mainly conducts the following research on Ubiquitous Networking and Security Issues, but the topic is not limited to the followings.

  • Computing Platform & Data Science

    • - Cloud Computing - Theory and Design (Open Stack, Cloud Stack)
    - Cloud Data Centre Simulator with Virtual Machines (e.g., Google Trace)
    - Multi-Core based Concurrent System (e.g., Scala, Clojure, Erlang, Elixir, Haskell, Rust, Go)
    - Distributed P2P and BitCoin Network System (e.g., Petri Nets Theory)

  • Network Systems and Protocols

    • - Internet of Things (IoT), BitCoin Protocol & Block Chain
    - Protocols and Platforms for Mobile Computing (e.g., Mobile IPv6, IMS)
    - Internet Multimedia Communications and QoS (e.g., SIP, VoLTE)
    - Telecommunications & Performance Evaluation (e.g., Queueing Theory)

  • System and Network Security - Attacks & Defenses

    • - Cyber Forensics and Cyber Cash (e.g., BitCoin Transaction Tracking)
    - IoT Security & Cloud Security
    - Wireless & Mobile Security
    - Cryptography (e.g., Number Theory and Abstract Algebra)

  • Quantum Computing

    • - Quantum Computer Simulation
    - Quantum Information Processing & Teleportation
    - Quantum Key Distribution (QKD) Protocols (BB84, B92, E91)
    - Quantum Mechanics (e.g., Linear Algebra)

  • Research References (Uk & US University)

    • - Data Science (Big Data)
    - Digital Forensics and Cyber Security
    - Telecommunications (IoT)
